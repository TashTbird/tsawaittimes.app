Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

TSA Wait Times ("the App") is operated by Apps4Us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Wait-time reports. When you submit a wait-time report, we store the checkpoint, reported wait in minutes, and an anonymous device identifier. We do not collect your name, email, or any account credentials — the App has no sign-up or login.

Location. With your permission, the App accesses your device location solely to show nearby airports. Location data is processed on-device and is never sent to our servers.

Notification token. If you enable spike alerts, the App requests a push-notification token from the operating system. This token is stored locally on your device.

Preferences. Settings such as "I have TSA PreCheck" and favorite airports are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. They are never transmitted.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Aggregate wait-time reports to compute and display estimated wait times for each checkpoint.
  • Deliver local push notifications when a favorited airport's wait time exceeds your chosen threshold.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase (database and real-time updates) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Expo / EAS (app build and update delivery) — expo.dev/privacy

We do not sell, share, or rent your data to any third parties for advertising or marketing.

4. Data Retention

Individual wait-time reports are retained for 90 days to compute rolling averages, then automatically deleted. Aggregated checkpoint statistics are kept indefinitely but contain no personal data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Choices

  • You may decline location access; the Nearby tab will simply be unavailable.
  • You may disable notifications in your device settings at any time.
  • You may delete the App to remove all locally stored data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.

8. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@tsawaittimes.app.
